HCI Project

Design principles

A variety of design principles were adopted to design a highly usable site.

In terms of visibility, we choose to employ a permanent navigation bar always at the top of the page for easy navigation.

For feedback, we aim to always provide feedback to users where necessary for e.g. notifying them of the reason behind the failure of creation of an account.

To achieve consistency, we chose to adopt a site structure similar to existing solutions such as Kaggle. Also, the design of basic interface controls are similar to popular websites such as boxing up buttons.
